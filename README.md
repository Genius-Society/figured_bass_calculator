Figured Bass Calculator is a system of musical notation originating in the Baroque period, used to guide the performance and arrangement of harmonic music. It uses numbers above the notes to indicate intervals above the root of a chord, and numbers below to indicate intervals below the root, thus indicating the structure and arrangement of chords in harmonic music. This flexible system of notation was widely used in Baroque music and continued to develop and evolve in subsequent musical periods, becoming an important part of Western classical music theory.
